How to prove Girl=Evil

发表于 2023-10-05 本文字数： 282 阅读时长 ≈ 1 分钟

\(\because To\ chase\ a\ girl\ needs\ time\ and\ money.\)

\(\therefore Girl\ =\ Time\times Money\)

\(\because time\ is\ money\)

\(\therefore Girl\ =\ Money^2\)

\(\because money\ is\ the\ \textbf{root}\ of\ evil.\)

\(\therefore Girl\ =\ (\sqrt{Evil})^2\)

\(\therefore Girl\ =\ Evil\)

重点词解释：

chase n. 追求

root n. 根；根源

evil adj. 邪恶的
